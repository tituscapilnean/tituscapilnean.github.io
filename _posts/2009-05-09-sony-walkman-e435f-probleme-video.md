Nervi, stres – mi-am luat un sony ca sa nu am parte de ele. Pe naiba, am incercat sa convertesc video-uri pentru el si…surpriza – mi-am pierdut jumatate de ora degeaba!

Abia dupa ce am scormonit prin formumuri am dat de :

download SUPER  
set container = mp4  
video codec = mpeg-4 @ h264/avc  
audio codec = aac lc  
video size = 320×240  
aspect ratio = 4:3 / 11:9 , your choice depending on the source video  
frame/sec = depends on the source vid/your choice  
bitrate = tech. spec for your model says mpeg-4 max 1024kbps, h264 max 768

check the attachment for extra setting you need to set if you’re encoding to h264/avc
